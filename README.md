# Data_projects_TripleTen
Projects I completed while attending the TripleTen Business Intelligence Analytics Bootcamp.  
Each project applies real analytical workflows — from data cleaning and feature engineering to visualization, dashboarding, and executive‑level storytelling.

| Project number | Title | Description |
|----------------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | [Manhattan Vacation Rental Market Analysis](./Manhattan_Vacation_Rental_Market_Analysis) | Analyzed Manhattan’s vacation rental market using advanced Excel techniques, including data cleaning, feature engineering, and pivot‑based revenue modeling. Identified top‑performing neighborhoods, evaluated revenue patterns by bedroom count, and delivered a scalable investment strategy focused on 1–2 bedroom units in high‑demand areas. |
| 2 | [E-Commerce Conversion & Cohort Analysis](./E_Commerce_Conversion_Cohort_Analysis) | Built a full conversion funnel and monthly retention cohorts using event‑level logs. Quantified drop‑off between funnel stages, identified the largest friction point between product views and cart opens, and revealed consistent post‑purchase retention decay. Delivered recommendations to strengthen mid‑funnel engagement and early‑stage reactivation. |
| 3 | [Superstore Performance & Recovery Strategy](./Superstore_Performance_Recovery_Strategy) | Developed a multi‑sheet Tableau analysis diagnosing profit centers, loss‑makers, advertising opportunities, and return‑rate patterns. Identified high‑margin sub‑categories, products to discontinue, and state‑month combinations with the strongest advertising ROI. Delivered a recovery plan focused on margin improvement and targeted marketing. |
| 4 | [Superstore – Understanding return patterns across categories, states and time](./Superstore_Understanding_Return_PatternS)
| Created a Tableau Story analyzing return behavior across customers, states, categories, and time. Identified high‑return customer segments, regional hotspots, seasonal spikes, and sub‑categories driving disproportionate return activity. Provided targeted recommendations to reduce avoidable returns and improve operational efficiency. |
