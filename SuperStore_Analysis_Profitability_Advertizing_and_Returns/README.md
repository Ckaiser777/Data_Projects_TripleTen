
# SuperStore Analysis – Profitability, Advertizing, and Returns


## Project Overview
This Tableau project analyzes Superstore operations to identify profit centers, major loss-makers, advertising opportunities, and return-rate patterns. The goal is to provide clear, data-driven recommendations that improve profitability and help prevent continued financial decline.

---

## Project Structure
- **Profits & Losses Analysis:**
  - Identified the biggest profit centers and largest loss-making sub-categories.
  - Evaluated P&L by region and sub-category to find where to invest and where to cut.
- **Products to Discontinue:**
  - Flagged individual products that consistently generate negative profit.
  - Quantified total losses from underperforming items.
- **Advertising Recommendations:**
  - Analyzed average monthly profit by state.
  - Identified state–month combinations with the strongest advertising potential.
- **Returned Items Analysis:**
  - Highlighted products and customers with unusually high return rates.
  - Compared profit vs. return rate across states.
- **Dashboard:** Combined all visuals into an executive-ready Tableau dashboard.

---

## Key Insights
- Copiers in the West and East regions are the strongest profit centers.
- Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies consistently generate negative profit and should be discontinued or re-priced.
- Vermont (December), Rhode Island (March), and Indiana (October) offer the best advertising opportunities based on average monthly profit.
- Several products and customers show unusually high return rates, signaling quality or expectation issues.
- States with high profit and low return rates should be prioritized, while low-profit, high-return states should be reevaluated.
